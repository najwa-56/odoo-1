Features

  • Auto focus barcode input field in scan success or failure.
  • Easy to search documents globally using the Barcode.
  • When you scan the barcode the barcode will automatically added to the barcode field inside the product.
  • QR code scanner option added.
  • Easy to add products in sale order, purchase order, customer invoices, vendor bills, delivery order, incoming order, etc by barcode scanner.
  • Barcodes eliminate the possibility of human error.
