Point of Sales - POS Price Look Up Code Odoo Apps

POS Search PLU Number Odoo App for international supermarkets offers an inventory management solution, and one of its standout features is the seamless POS (Point of Sale) search functionality based on PLU (Price Look Up) numbers. Users can manage their inventory and shopping experience for their customers. One of the best customizable settings in this app is the ability for users to enable or disable PLU search directly from the POS configuration.

Each product in the inventory can be associated with a unique PLU code, typically consisting of 4 or 5 digits. When a user needs to locate a particular product quickly, they can simply enter the PLU number into the search bar on the POS screen. The system will then rapidly retrieve the product information.
